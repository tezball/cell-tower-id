Google Play Services Location

Used by Cell Tower ID for the FusedLocationProviderClient API,
which supplies GPS coordinates that are paired with cell tower
observations during a collection session.

Distributed by Google LLC under the Google Play Services Terms of
Service:

    https://developers.google.com/android/guides/overview

Use of the Google Play Services SDK is also governed by the
Google APIs Terms of Service:

    https://developers.google.com/terms

Cell Tower ID does not transmit any location data, cell tower data,
or device identifiers off the device. The Play Services Location
client is used purely for on-device positioning.
